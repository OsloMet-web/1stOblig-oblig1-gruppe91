ARGUMENTS AGAINST GROUP WORK

Risk of repeating the same information inside a project.

Delivering clear and obvious tasks to each other can be hard to do inside a group

People in the group may not exactly understand their task

Tasks can end up being repeated. Several people doing the same thing is often not efficient or effective.

ARGUMENTS FOR GROUP WORK

Increasing creativity of the project you are working on. The reason why working in a group increases the creativity, is because when you do something on your own, it is easy to find a solution and stick to it. When in a group, you will find different solutions, and together decide which one is best suited to solve the problem.

Working in a group allows for tasks to be divided among the group members, which can significantly improve the pace at which tasks are completed.
