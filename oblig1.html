ARGUMENT AGAINST GROUP WORK

Risk of repeating the same information inside a project. To be deliver clear and obvious tasks to each other can be hard to do inside a group People in the group does not exactly understand their task information can end up being repeated and people doing the same thing is not efficient or effective.

ARGUMENTS FOR GROUP WORK

Increasing creativity of the project you are working on. The reason why working in a group increase the creativity is because, when you do something on your own it is very easy to just find a solution and stick with that but in a group, you will find different solution and then decide which ones better.
